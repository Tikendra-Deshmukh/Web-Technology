New Delhi

New Delhi, the capital and the third largest city of India, is a fusion of the ancient and the modern. The remains of Muslim Dynasties with their architectural delights give the majestic ambiance of the bygone era.

On the other side, New Delhi, the imperial city built by the British, reflects the fast-paced present. The most fascinating of all is the character of Delhi, which varies from the 13th-century mausoleum of the Lodi kings to ultra-modern glass skyscrapers.
